### Tracking Technologies & Cookies
We use Cookies and similar tracking tech to keep tabs on activity on our Service and stash away certain info. The tracking technologies we use include beacons, tags, and scripts, all for collecting and tracking info, plus improving and analyzing our Service. The tech we might use includes: 👇

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a tiny file dropped onto your Device. You can tell your browser to say "nope" to all Cookies or to give you a heads-up when one's being sent. But hey, if you don't play along with Cookies, you might not be able to use certain parts of our Service. Unless you've tweaked your browser settings to reject Cookies, our Service is likely to use 'em.
- **Web Beacons**: Some parts of our Service and our emails might contain small electronic files called Web Beacons (also known as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs). These little guys let the Company (for example) count users who have visited those pages or opened an email, and for other website stats (like noting the popularity of a certain section and double-checking system and server integrity).

Cookies can be either "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies chill on your personal computer or mobile device even when you go offline, while Session Cookies get the boot the moment you close your web browser.

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for these purposes: 👇

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are absolutely crucial for giving you the services available through the Website and letting you use some of its cool features. They help authenticate users and prevent fraudulent use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you've asked for can't be delivered, and we only use these Cookies to hook you up with those services. 🙌

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are used to figure out if users have accepted the use of cookies on the Website.

- **Functional Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies let us remember the choices you make when you use the Website, like recalling your login deets or language preferences. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a more personalized experience and save you the hassle of re-entering your preferences every single time you use the Website. 😌

Wanna know more about the Cookies we use and your choices regarding them? Swing by our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy.
